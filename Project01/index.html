A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Born on 15th October 1931 at Rameswaram in Tamil Nadu, Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam specialized in Aeronautical Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology. He played a pivotal role in developing India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III), launching the Rohini satellite into orbit in 1980. After contributing significantly at ISRO, he led the Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme (IGMDP) at DRDO, overseeing AGNI and PRITHVI missile development. He was a key figure in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests and advanced the nation's self-reliance in defense systems.
